What’s
A Macro?

Macros put you in real-world contexts where you work alongside your classmates in teams to solve actual challenges that organizations in the community are facing. These experiences account for nearly 60% of your schedule and are designed to help you develop the creative problem solving skills so critical to professional success today.

In any given year, you could, say, get the hang of geometry while you help design a sports stadium, or get a handle on differential calculus while you build, test, then launch a rocket.
